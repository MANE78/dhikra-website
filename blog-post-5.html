Morning and evening adhkar are a collection of supplications and glorifications taken from the Noble Quran and the pure Prophetic Sunnah, which Muslims are recommended to maintain at specific times of the day. These adhkar are not mere words to be recited, but rather a complete spiritual program that connects the Muslim with their Lord and protects them from evils.

  • Morning Adhkar: From Fajr until Duha (around 10 AM)
  • Evening Adhkar: From Asr until Maghrib

Islamic law chose these two times because they represent the beginning and end of daily activity, so the Muslim remains protected throughout their day by remembering Allah Almighty.

Dhikr brings tranquility to the heart as Allah Almighty said: "Verily, in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest." The Muslim who maintains morning and evening adhkar finds in their heart an indescribable peace and comfort.
